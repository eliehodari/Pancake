Classic pancake recipe

Cooking time is fifteen miniutes

Serves four people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of Baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • Tablespoon melted butter

Instructions

  1. In large bowl , whisk floar, sugar, baking soda ,baking soda and salt
  2. In another bowl, mix butter milk, egg, and melted butter
  3. pour the wet ingreadients into the dry ingredients and stir until combined
  4. heat a non-stick pan over medium heat ligtly grease with butter
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan and cook until bubble form on surface
  6. Flip pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter serve warm with topping of chocolate
